How it works

Diagnose, rewind, test

The same three steps for every motor, whether it's a table fan or a factory compressor.

Step 01

Diagnose

We check the winding, bearings, and insulation to find the actual fault before quoting anything.

Step 02

Rewind & repair

Burnt windings are stripped and rewound with 100% copper wire; bearings and seals replaced as needed.

Step 03

Test & hand over

Every motor is load-tested for RPM, heat, and vibration before it leaves the workshop.
